About Me

My name is Julien Dupas (located on the right) and I have had a passion for filmmaking for

several years now alongside my twin brother Liam (located on the left) . I enjoy watching

movies, working out, making films, and hanging out with friends. Regarding filmmaking, my

main passion is Cinematography. And for those who don't know, a Cinematographer controls

all the lighting, camera movement, and composition for film. This is the area I want to

specialize in and have a passion for.



Although I have not had any internships yet, I have had a lot of professional experiences.

Another professional experience I had was during the Summer. I went to Publix to find a

product to film for a spec advertisement. I found a product called Lemon Perfect, and

decided to film a product commercial for them. I did some research on the company and

found out that they are local to where I live. I proceeded to email the CEO of the company

and asked if he was interested in my brother and I filming a commercial for free. Within

a week, the CEO responded and he connected me to his brand manager from which he

scheduled us a virtual meeting. Soon later we went to the company's office and talked to the

social media manager. After the meeting we met the CEO. Not only did this overall experience

help me learn so much about the business world, but also opened my eyes for future

opportunities. Regarding hobbies, I really enjoy photography. I find photographs to be very

calming and one of the best ways to live in the moment. When I take photos, I don't think

about anything else besides the shot I am getting and I find that very peaceful.

I also like color grading my photos to look like they were shot on film stock.
